template<typename Particle_T >
std::vector<Particle_T> Probability::resampleLowVariance ( const std::vector< std::pair< Particle_T, double > > &  aWeightedParticles)

Samples a vector (with replacement) using a low-variance strategy.

New samples are included with probability proportional to their weight, with repeats allowed. This sampler is efficient: O(particles).

Reference: Probabilistic Robotics, Ch. 4.3, Table 4.4 (pg. 110)

Template Parameters
Particle_TType of the particle being re-sampled
Parameters
